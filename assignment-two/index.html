    Chocoramo

    What is?

    The famous chocolate-covered cake had humble beginnings as a dessert that Ana Luisa Camacho made for her husband Rafael Molano, who worked at the Bavaria company, as part of their daily elevenses. Its popularity grew when Rafael's colleagues started asking him to sell the same dessert to them for a few pesos in the dining room of the company.

    Chocoramo, the delicious chocolate-covered cake with its unmistakable orange wrapper, has captured the hearts of Colombians and has made a mark on the country's culinary history.

    The little brother?

    Yes! it is called "Barrita Chocoramo" and it is a smaller version of the original Chocoramo. It is a snack that is perfect for a quick bite or to satisfy a craving. It is also a great option for kids' lunchboxes or as a snack for school or work. The principle is the same, a chocolate-covered cake, but in a smaller size and in the middle, it has a layer of blackberry and strawberry jam.

    How to enjoy it?

    There are many ways to enjoy a Chocoramo. You can eat it as a snack, as a dessert, or as a treat. You can also enjoy it with a cup of coffee, a glass of milk, or a cup of hot chocolate. You can eat it on its own or you can use it as an ingredient in other recipes. You can also enjoy it with friends, family, or on your own. The possibilities are endless!
